The Meaning

Arshaan translates to "Bravery", "Honor", or "Strength". It embodies the archetype of a courageous leader who stands firmly for justice and integrity.

A Union of Cultures

The name Arshaan is a linguistic bridge, resonating across ancient civilizations and modern hearts.

Arabic

Intertwined with meanings of "Dignity" and "Status," mirroring the phonetic beauty of high-ranking virtues.

Persian

The direct lineage to the "Kayanian" kings, representing the "Arsh" (The Throne) and a noble spirit.

Indian

A modern favorite in the subcontinent, representing a "Pure" and "Radiant" soul that illuminates its surroundings.
